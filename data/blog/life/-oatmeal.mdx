# Overminute Oats

My faster take on overnight oats.

## Ingredients

- oats
- milk powder
- toppings:
  - frozen berries
  - cinnamon
  - sesame seeds
  - honey

## Recipe

1. Mix oats and milk powder in a cup.
2. Add water.
3. Mix a lot.
4. Add toppings.
